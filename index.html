About Us

Washington's VSL Hair Design is a craft hair salon that offers trendy and fashion-forward hair designs. This salon has everything you need to make a bold fashion statement with your hair. The stylists at this salon are here to provide you with creative, professional and top-of-the-line services, including hair cuts, color, highlights, and balayage.
